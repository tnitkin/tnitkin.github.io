About

I’m a results-driven Data Scientist with expertise in Python, Power BI, SQL, and machine learning techniques, including large language models (LLMs), XGBoost, and TensorFlow. I’ve delivered predictive models, dashboards, and data pipelines across corporate, nonprofit, and health sectors.

I currently lead analytics at IntelliDyne, and previously directed global analytics at World Vision and Medical Teams International. I also co-founded Inspire Ethiopia, where I apply data science to capture results and model potential opportunities to create increased access to education. I’m passionate about using data for insight and impact.
